Content of the day : 
Prompt Engineering simply means learning how to "ask" an AI tool the right way so it gives you the best possible answer.
Think of it like giving instructions to a very smart but very literal assistant — the clearer and more specific your instructions, the better the result you get back.
AI tools like Claude don't read your mind. They respond based on exactly what you type. A vague question gets a vague answer. 
A clear, well-structured prompt gets a clear, useful, and often surprisingly powerful answer. 
Good prompt engineering is the difference between AI feeling "okay" and AI feeling like magic.

Key Learnings:
❌ Weak Prompt:

"Write something about marketing."
Result: A generic, surface-level paragraph that could apply to anyone, anywhere — not very useful.
✅ Improved Prompt:
"Act as a marketing strategist. Write a 100-word LinkedIn post for a small bakery business, explaining 3 simple ways to attract local customers using social media. 
Use a friendly, conversational tone."
