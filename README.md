My approach usually follows a cycle of curiosity, exploration, planning, building, and continuous improvement. I enjoy connecting technical knowledge with real-world impact, often using AI to enhance both my professional development and personal growth. What sets me apart is my tendency to seek deeper understanding rather than surface-level solutions.
